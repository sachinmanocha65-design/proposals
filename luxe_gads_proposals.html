The Strategy Pillars

A logic-driven approach to dominate the patient search journey.

01 Procedure Architecture

We will completely rebuild the account away from generic "All Services" campaigns. By creating dedicated campaigns for each core procedure (e.g., Breast, Face, Body Lipo, Wellness), we ensure that your budget is controlled at the service level, allowing us to scale high-margin surgeries without being drained by low-value traffic.

"Clean Data = Granular Budget Control"

02 Intent-Led Keywords

We focus exclusively on commercial and transactional queries. We use Exact and Phrase match types to target patients actively seeking a surgeon in Louisiana, avoiding those simply researching procedures or looking for DIY alternatives.

High Intent
'breast augmentation Alexandria LA'
Local Surgeon
'BBL surgeon near me'

03 Negative Framework

A robust negative keyword list is built before a single ad goes live. We proactively block informational queries ('what is lipo'), price shoppers ('cheapest botox'), student searches ('cosmetic nursing'), and geographic locations outside your Central Louisiana catchment area.

04 Value-Based Budgeting

Budget is allocated in proportion to business value. Tier 1 (Breast, BBL, Lipo) receives the largest share for maximum ROI. Tier 2 (Injectables, Wellness) receives a targeted allocation to drive volume and introduce new patients to the clinic.
